Why should we read?

  • Provides insight
  • Takes Your Mind Off Your Trouble
  • Reading Engages Your Imagination
  • Stronger Analytical Thinking Skills
  • Best friends of loneliness

But how can we develop a reading habit?

  • Start with a series. You will be up on your heels.
  • Read at Least 10-20 Pages a Day
  • Keep reading materials handy
  • Consider your hobbies and personal interests
  • Make list of too read books

Some books which will help to develop reading habit

  • The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald
  • The Catcher in the Rye by J. D. Salinger
  • The Kite Runner By Khaled Hosseini
  • Malgudi Days by R K Narayanan
  • Lolita by Vladimir Nabokov
